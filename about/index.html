About

Sahil is a co-founder of CPC Analytics, a data-driven consulting firm with offices in Berlin and Pune. As a part of CPC, he has consulted leading private and public sector organizations such as Siemens, Mitsubishi Materials, the World Health Organization and GIZ (German International Cooperation).

Prior to founding CPC, Sahil worked for GIZ (German International Cooperation) in Tanzania alongside the Prime Minister’s Office, to streamline the revenue collection process in Local Government Agencies. He has also worked on a European Union project in Morocco & Egypt, which aimed to create financially viable models for investment in the renewable energy sector.

Sahil has a Masters in Public Policy from the Hertie School, Berlin and an undergraduate in computer engineering which he pursued at the University of Pune, India and Ecoles des Mines, France. He is a PhD candidate at the Hertie School.
